Rosethorn Rules

🏰 Minecraft Realm

  • Respect Others — No griefing, stealing, or harassment.
  • Build Responsibly — Avoid lag machines or disruptive structures.
  • No Cheating — Mods or clients must be pre-approved.
  • Protect Nature — Replant trees, avoid large-scale destruction.
  • Report Issues — Reach out to staff if problems arise.

🕸️ Discord Server

  • Respect Everyone — Zero tolerance for hate speech or drama.
  • Stay Organized — Use correct channels for each topic.
  • No Spam — Avoid flooding messages, tags, or commands.
  • Keep Things Private — Don’t share DMs or sensitive info.
  • Follow Staff Guidance — Listen to moderators; they maintain peace.
